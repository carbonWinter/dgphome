AM, FM & Everything In-Between

Choosing the right channel can mean all the difference for your radio campaign’s success…

There are so many options as to how to advertise on the radio, and not everyone uses each channel. When marketing your practice, we keep track of which demographics listen to each type of station—AM, FM, XM, streaming—to ensure you’re always reaching the right potential patients with the right message. For example, if you have a children’s practice, airing your spot on Radio Disney during the early morning commute to school can be much more successful than a more general station later in the day.

We also understand that traditional AM and FM radio have their unique strengths, which we target. FM attracts over 220 million listeners weekly, allowing you to reach larger local audiences faster on a daily basis. However, AM radio is no slouch with well over 50 million listeners per week. AM listeners also tend to listen longer, and commercial-friendly talk radio means they’re often likelier to pay more attention longer than FM radio listeners. At Dental Game Plan, we take these differences into account from the start, so you always choose the best methods and channels for your practice’s growth.

Using Podcasts In Your Dental Marketing

See why podcast ad spots are the perfect blend of traditional radio and new media marketing…

While your competition (and other marketing companies) may only target traditional radio networks, they fail to realize that 32% of Americans listen to podcasts at least once a month. When do people listen to AM/FM radio the most?

People in the 15-24 range are most likely to listen while driving, while those 25-54 years old are most likely to listen during breakfast. Those in the 55+ range are most likely to listen in the mid-morning. This segmentation allows us to better target your practice’s radio ads, such as placing implant or denture ads mid-morning or cosmetic treatments earlier.
