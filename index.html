The Challenges of
Dental Marketing

Attracting new patients and growing your practice isn’t as simple as sending out fliers or making a website…

Dental marketing has gone through a major overhaul in recent years. Where before dentists could rely on recommendations, postcards, and the occasional radio or television commercial, a whole new side of marketing has appeared: online marketing. The most successful offices focus on both traditional and digital marketing to reach as many people as possible. Many offices have tried breaking through on their own with little to show for their efforts. Even going through a marketing agency can lead to paltry results. These companies don’t understand dentistry or the ins and outs of dental marketing.

There are 5 stages to a successful dental marketing campaign. You have the ad, the lead, the appointment, the show/no show, and treatment acceptance. Any problems in your marketing strategy during these stages leads to missed patients, lost revenue, and high costs. The right ads might not reach the right people. Your website might not have the right tools to get patients to call and schedule an appointment. Patients may book a visit but then never appear or decide not to get the filling or crown they need.
