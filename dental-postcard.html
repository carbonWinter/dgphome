How much does it cost to mail a postcard?

Mailing a postcard is extremely affordable and allows you to quickly reach a large number of people quickly. Every Door Direct Mail is a relatively inexpensive service that allows you to send a postcard to everyone within a certain geographical radius—or, more accurately, a USPS mailing route. This method is relatively inexpensive and lets you leave no stone unturned. With dental reactivation postcards, you have a set mailing list of existing patients. Regular postcards cost about 35 cents each to mail via USPS.

How frequently should I mail postcards?

It is recommended that you mail postcards to your prospective customers every month or two through your regular dental postcards mailers. However, the Rule of 7 states that you should send at least 7 postcards to potential new patients. While some people may call your office after the first postcard, this is the average number of times it takes for new patients to act. However, you want to be sure you’re not sending the same postcard every time with the same design and visuals.
