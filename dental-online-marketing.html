Google Adwords is the giant of search engine marketing, and we can help you

Banner Ads: Pictures Worth A Thousand Words

Visually-striking banner ads get the word out better than any paragraph or essay!


Dynamic and colorful banner ads are immediately more attention-grabbing than plain old text. If you’re not already using them to promote your practice, ask yourself why not. Banners can be placed anywhere you think a large concentration of users will roam, like news sites. Just like other forms of dental online marketing, these can be adjusted to target audiences with different interests, demographics, and more. If you don’t like the kind of crowd you’re drawing, banner ads can be adjusted to filter them out so that, like a sieve, you only get what (or who) you’re looking for.

What are the different types of online marketing?

The main types of internet marketing are social media marketing, influencer marketing, affiliate marketing, email marketing, content marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), and paid advertising. At Dental Game Plan, our primary dental online marketing services are social media, email, content, SEO, and paid advertising.

What are some disadvantages or pitfalls of online marketing?

Successfully marketing your dental practice online depends not only on modern technology (which can backfire due to technical difficulties, viruses, router failures, or security issues). It also relies on a clear knowledge of how the specific online dental market works. Many dental offices waste hundreds or thousands of dollars on ad revenue and digital marketing strategies that don’t take advantage of how dental patients search and find dental offices online. Dental marketing is a highly competitive field, and your competitors can quickly pick up any slack you leave behind.

Is online marketing preferred more than offline marketing?

While offline marketing channels like television, radio, and postcards are great at bringing in new patients, dental online marketing is becoming increasingly more popular and effective. The majority of dental patients are looking online to find their new dentist, so most practices are transitioning—if they haven’t already—to the online marketing world. Internet marketing also allows you to track your results and successes in real-time, which traditional marketing can’t do.
